Steps to Access Copilot Billing
Go to GitHub.com and sign in with the account you used to subscribe to Copilot.

In the upper-right corner, click your profile picture → Settings.

In the left sidebar, select Billing and plans.

Under Plans and usage, you’ll see your Copilot subscription details (tier, renewal date, payment method).

You can also download invoices/receipts from the Billing history section.
